The Flaws of Instant Communication.

Before I sell you on digital hermiticism I want to talk about why I think instant communication is flawed.

The Default Behaviour of Instant Communication

Impulsivity.

Instant Communication allows us to communicate instantly.

Like the addict who reaches for their vape in the morning, the lack of friction allows us to engage in impulsive and often inappropriate behaviour.

It is the kind of thing that causes pre-mature declarations of love, the slinging of insults, irrational arguements, "crash outs" and more

The keen reader may realise that these are all features of regular in person conversation.

Why should we abandon the digital version of this?

The Dehumanisation of Digital Communication

Real life conversations have invisible cues that help inform how to act.

When conversing digitally you can only see the persons text. You fail to see their face and posture and tone of voice and many other things that help inform how you should phrase your rebuttal.

You no longer are talking to a human but engaging in combat with a faceless enemy.

I have qualms with in person conversation as well but that is for another blog.

Smoke and Mirrors Connection

Social media like Instagram and Discord both make you feel like you have friends.

Maybe this is a personal issue but like what are friends?

If it is people whos company you enjoy then I have many friends.

If it is people who you trust and can confide in then I have few maybe even 0 friends.

Eitherway when I engage in conversation in a server I feel connected but that feeling fades in an instant.

It feels less like I am a person and more like content on a persons feed.

To an extent I like that! I like that people like me around! But its like now theres this expectation. I am supposed to be funny or insightful or pretentious or or or-

I realise I got a bit personal there but like you see how instant communication exacerbates these insecurities of mine right?

Cloaks and Daggers

Instant Communication facilitates bullying and the spreading of rumors.

This one also hits a bit close to home because I have had negative experiences of rumors spreading about me in late night discord calls.

Being talked about and not to hurts. I know what it is like on both fronts and I don't like it.

The shortlist

Those are the ones that are at the top of my head. I am sure there is more (the financial incentive to make users addicted to your app and how that fucks with your users is another example.) but I believe these ones are potent enough to convince someone that digital Instant Communication is flawed.

So solutions! I believe that all of these can be mitigated through working on yourself but I think its alot easier to work on yourself when the thing ruining you isn't accessible.

Digital Hermiticism: Becoming Unreachable.

I think most people have a vague idea of what I mean when I say "Digital Hermiticism"

The truth is that is a bad term.

Because I mean hermit as in eremitism not the philosophical "Hermeticism"

I just wanted a nice flashy title. So to be clear:

I am talking specifically about becoming a digital hermit

The Basics

Its pretty simple!

Isolating yourself from the artificial connection that digital communication has a couple benefits (and also some risks)

If the digital connections are supplementing the desire for real and honest connection then the cessation of those digital connections will force you to actually feel the loneliness

The hope is that it will push me to hang out with people in real life more.

I want to know the people I call my friends in the real world.

I want to make new friends who exist in physical space

Degrees of Severity

Being a digital recluse has degrees of severity.

You can abandon all forms of remote contact

You can abandon specifically instant communication

You can abandon only certain types of instant communication such as internet communication

The point (for me at least) is that you remove the parts that damage you so you have space to grow.

For me and for now I want to stop specifically conversation happening in digital space.

So instant communication is fine for certain things like organising social events or emergency contact or finding someone if they are lost but anything that resembles a casual or serious conversation is terminated.

If you want to talk to me about something serious or if you want to spend time with me you gotta book an appointment.

We gotta get lunch or go to a park or climb a mountain or something.

DEFCON 3

In my obsidian vault I have a note called "Social DEFCON System" and right now I am at DEFCON 3.

I have to recognise that this "Digital Hermiticism" is a crutch and a defense mechanism

I know that I have to eventually walk it back.

Right now this is where I am at:

  • All conversations that use Digital Instant Communication must have a distinct purpose
  • Once that distinct purpose is over, the groupchat or server is left.
  • Conversations that are not mere exchanges of data do not happen through digital instant communication
    • Conversations about deciding a date to hangout are fine
    • Conversations that are for fun or have deep emotion behind them are not

So yeah, thats where I am at right now.
